Twebble


Twebble is the first full-featured Twitter client for the Pebble. It works with SDK 2.0 and provides a unique way to check Twitter from your wrist.

Twebble is currently in BETA and it'll be released in the Pebble App Store as soon as it is available.

Get beta

It requires Pebble SDK 2.0 beta 4 or latest
